Why LastMile?

How easy is it to travel to/from a town located more than 100KMs from a large airport? Mumbai airport to Shirdi, Delhi to Agra, or Bangalore to Mysore. It is not the distance, but the lack of reliable transport that ruins your journey. We aim to solve it by providing a share model by aggregating passengers and travel operators.

Aligned to flights

From the LastMile App, locate your flight and then choose the route you are travelling on. Submit your trip details and wait. We will align you with the right co-passenger and provide you with travel offers you can't resist. Upon your arrival, get into your pre-arranged vehicle and zoom to your destination.

Plan early

Plan your last mile trip when you book your flight tickets. You can submit your trip on LastMile two months prior to your schedule. Early planning helps you get the best travel deals and good co-travellers. You get notified when another traveller submits his/her trip that is aligned with yours. Start chatting with them on the App.

Sleep peacefully

Don't worry about how to get to your final destination. Submitting your trip helps you reduce your travel cost, make some good friends on the way, and most importantly take the worry out of your mind. Irrespective of the time of day or night, you will have a transport option available to your destination.

Blink & You're Home!

Experience the simplicity. Locate your arriving or departing flights by choosing the date and flight type. Select your route and submit the trip. Sit back, relax and let us pair you with the best co-traveller and provide you the best ride deals.
